Prima the leader of the 13 and the very 1st Prime. Hence why I call him: Big Boss The 1st Matrix bearer Prima is a warrior of light and was the most similar to their creator, Primus out of all of his siblings. Cast in the mold of Primus himself, Prima did have a strong verdict that he was always right did make him hard to deal with at times. However he was thankfully self aware enough so as to not be a Pompous Gas-Bag Prime (Sentinel I'm looking at you *insert surprised Sentichu face here*). He was also the 1st wielder of the Star Saber. This mystic sword can only be wielded by a Prime. In an episode of Transformers Prime the Decepticons unearth the glowing blue blade with the hilt jutting out of a rock and nobody except Optimus Prime could pull the sword from the stone. So of course I'm gonna call the original wielder of Cybertronian Excalibur Cyber King Arthur!



My Depiction of the Character
Prima is a natural born leader. Strong, brave, the physical embodiment of good and light. He cares about his people although at times can be a little, weeeeellllll, frustrating. He sees everything in black and white. Shades of gray are a concept that he can never seem to grasp. That isn't to say he is a fool. Quite the contrary in fact this bot is a wise leader with a very solid sense of what is right. He may cause his siblings to want to tear out their - well, I guess they don't really have hair or at least most of them don't but you get what I mean - but they know they can rely on him to be there and fight along side them when the time comes. A literal white knight they know that Prima will never give up and run away. 1 of the noblest of mechs, and filled with honor, he will fight and fight until the bitter end. A mech truly worthy of the Matrix, Prima serves as a role model for the many Primes and leaders of Cybertron to come after.
